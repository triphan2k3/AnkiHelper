Ý nghĩa của carbo-loading trong tiếng Anh

carbo-loading
noun [ U ]
uk
/ˈkɑː.bəʊˌləʊ.dɪŋ/ us
/ˈkɑːr.boʊˌloʊ.dɪŋ/
(also carb loading, carb-loading, carbohydrate loading, uk/kɑː.bəʊˈhaɪ.dreɪt ˌləʊ.dɪŋ/ us/kɑːr.boʊˈhaɪ.dreɪt ˌloʊ.dɪŋ/)
Add to word list Add to word list
the practice of eating a lot of carbohydrates (= foods that provide the body with energy) so that the body stores as much energy as possible, especially before taking part in a long race or a competitive game :
Carbo-loading is important, and some players consume 5,000 calories a day at specific points in the week.
Carb loading can be helpful for sports like long distance running, cycling, and swimming.
I do carb-loading to keep my electrolytes at a certain level.
He alternated a low-carbohydrate diet with periods of carbohydrate loading.

  • The day before the race, he had a carbo-loading pasta dinner with his friends.
